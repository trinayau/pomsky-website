Would a Pomsky be right for you?

Pomskies are Pomeranian-Husky mixes which have become incredibly popular over the last seven years. They are beautiful and lovely dogs known to look like miniature Huskies, but with a smaller size suited for city living. If you are here, you probably have already fallen in love with their cute looks and sweet nature. Scroll down to find out more about why Pomskies may not be right for you.

Facts about Pomskies that may surprise you

You must think long and hard about where Pomskies will fit into your life due to the reasons below:

Pomskies are high energy

If you have a busy schedule, live alone, or can't spend several hours a day with your puppy, then a Pomsky may not be for you. They need at least 1 long walk a day. If left alone for long periods they can become bored and destructive.

Learn more

They shed a lot

Pomskies have a thick double layered coat. This coat will "blow out" twice a year and sheds year round. If you don't like to constantly groom, vacuum, and clean dog hair out of the corners of the couch, then consider a different breed.

Learn more

Pomskies can be Stubborn

Owners need to be firm trainers and set the tone from the very beginning with their puppy. Inconsistency can lead to confusion and frustration. Pomskies prefer to have a job to do and love to be trained to work or do tricks. If you can't devote the time properly training your pomsky, or don't feel like you can be firm with them, then consider a different breed.
