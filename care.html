How to Care for Pomskies

It is vital to take your Pomsky puppy to your veterinarian for a check-up in the first two days after taking them home for the first time. This is to identify any health issues which can easily be resolved if diagnosed early, such as parvovirus.

How to Train Pomskies

Pomskies are a highly intelligent dog breed and extremely sociable from its combined Husky and Pomeranian heritage.

If they are not given enough attention through play and training, they will become bored and destructive. Consider enrolling your Pomsky in puppy school between 13-16 weeks old.
